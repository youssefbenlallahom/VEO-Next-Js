Responsibilities

Data Collection, Analysis and Reporting:

Collect, clean, and validate data from multiple sources (databases, APIs, business systems, surveys).

Analyze large datasets to identify trends, patterns, and correlations that drive business insights.

Prepare and deliver reports, dashboards, and data visualizations to communicate findings to stakeholders in a clear and actionable manner.

Trend Identification, Forecasting and Benchmarking:

Apply statistical methods and predictive modeling techniques to forecast key business metrics.

Identify business performance trends and anomalies to support proactive decision-making.

Conduct benchmarking studies to compare internal performance against industry standards and competitors.

KPI Development and Monitoring:

Develop, track, and maintain key performance indicators (KPIs) relevant to business objectives.

Partner with stakeholders to define metrics that measure operational efficiency, customer behavior, and strategic outcomes.

Process Improvement and Optimization:

Translate analytical insights into recommendations that improve workflows, resource allocation, and overall business performance.

Automate recurring reports and dashboards to increase efficiency and reduce manual effort.

Collaborate with cross-functional teams to optimize data pipelines, ETL processes, and data governance.

Strategic Support and Decision-Making:

Provide data-driven support to leadership for strategic planning, market analysis, and business growth initiatives.

Contribute to project evaluations through cost-benefit analysis, ROI calculations, and scenario modeling.

Support change management by assessing the impact of new systems, processes, or market conditions using data insights.

Technology, Tools, and Data Management:

Ensure data quality, integrity, and compliance with company standards and regulations (e.g., GDPR).

Support the evaluation, implementation, and maintenance of BI tools, databases, and data visualization platforms.

Develop SQL queries, scripts, and automated processes for data extraction, transformation, and analysis.

Requirements

Minimum 3 years of experience in data analysis, business intelligence, or a related field.

Strong proficiency in SQL, Excel, and data visualization tools (Power BI, Tableau, or similar).

Experience with statistical analysis and forecasting techniques (Python/R is a plus).

Knowledge of data warehousing, ETL processes, and database management.

Strong problem-solving and critical-thinking skills with attention to detail.

Excellent communication skills in French & English (professional level).

Ability to work cross-functionally and translate data findings into actionable business strategies.
